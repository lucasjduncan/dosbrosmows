What We Offer

The Flows, explained.

Every plan is built around showing up consistently and making your yard look great. Here's exactly what you get with each.

Budget Flow

The Budget Flow covers the basics — mowing and blowing — done right every single time. Clean cuts, clean property. And when fall rolls around, we'll take care of the leaves too at no extra charge.

  • Mowing
  • Blowing
  • Leaf blowing (fall months, included)

Classic Flow

The Classic Flow is our most popular plan for a reason. Full mow, edge, and blow every visit — plus weed spray to keep the property clean, leaf blowing in the fall, and a free gift just for being a customer.

  • Mowing
  • Edging
  • Blowing
  • Weed spray (included)
  • Leaf blowing (fall months, included)
  • Free gift (included)

Extra Flow

Everything in the Classic Flow, plus hedge trimming. If you want the whole property dialed in — not just the grass — the Extra Flow is it.

  • Mowing
  • Edging
  • Blowing
  • Hedge trimming
  • Weed spray (included)
  • Leaf blowing (fall months, included)
  • Free gift (included)
